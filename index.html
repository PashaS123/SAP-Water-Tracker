Small choices. Big impact.

The average American home uses about 82 gallons of water per person each day. By understanding your habits and making simple changes, you can reduce waste and make a real difference.

Household Survey

Tell us about your home.

All questions are required so the estimate is based on a complete household picture. You do not need to know model names; choose the closest match.

Required questions answered 0%
1 Household Size

This turns total household use into gallons per person per day.

2 Showers

A showerhead uses water each minute. Pick the closest type; the calculator uses a reasonable estimate.

3 Toilets

Toilets are estimated by type and how often they are used. If you do not know the model, choose the closest description.

4 Faucets

Faucets are estimated by gallons per minute, but you only need to choose the closest faucet or aerator type.

5 Dishwasher / Dish Washing

Dishwashers are estimated by gallons per cycle. Hand washing is estimated higher when the tap runs often.

6 Laundry

Washing machines use different amounts of water depending on type and whether loads are full.

7 Leaks + Habits

Leaks can quietly waste a lot of water. A running toilet is especially important to fix.
